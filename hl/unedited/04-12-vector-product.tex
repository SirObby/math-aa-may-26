\obeylines
P 614-620
$a\times b=\begin{vmatrix}i, j, k \\ a_x a_y a_z \\ b_x b_y b_z\end{vmatrix}$
$=ia_yb_z+j_azb_x+ka_xb_y-b_xa_yk-b_ya_zi-b_za_xj$
$=|a||b|sin(\alpha)\cdot n$
where n is a normal vector to the plane of a and b.
Properties:
$a\times b=-(b\times a)$
$a\times (b\times c)=(a\times b)\times c$
$a\times (b+c)=a\times b+a\times c$
where $\times$ is called vector product or cross product.
Example:
