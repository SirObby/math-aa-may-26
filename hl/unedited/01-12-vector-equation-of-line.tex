p 605
Line passes points $A=(x_a,x_y,z_a)$ and $B=(x_b,y_b,z_b)$
direction vector of line is AB or BA.
line has equation $l=OA+t\cdot AB$
where $t\in\mathbb{R}$
l=$\begin{vmatrix}x_a \\ y_a \\ z_a \end{vmatrix}+t\cdot \begin{vmatrix}x_b-x_a\\y_b-y_a\\z_b-z_a\end{vmatrix}$ is vector form
and parameter form$\begin{cases}x=x_a+t(x_b-x_a)\\y=y_a+t(y_b-y_a)\\z=z_a+t(z_b-z_a)\end{cases}$
solve all rows for $t=t=t$ and move all terms into middle $=0$ is called cartesian form of equation of line.
