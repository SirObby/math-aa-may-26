 $\begin{cases}adx +bdy =ed\\bcx+bdy=fb \end{cases}$
subtracty=....
$(ad-bc)\cdot x=ed-fb$
$x=\frac{ed-fb}{ad-bc}$ and eliminate of x
 $\begin{cases}a\cdot x+b\cdot y=e |\cdot c\\c\cdot x+d\cdot y=f |\cdot a\end{cases}$
$\begin{cases}acx+bcy=ec\\cx+ady=af\end{cases}$
$0+(bc-ad)y=ec-af$
$y=\frac{ec-af}{bc-ad}$
