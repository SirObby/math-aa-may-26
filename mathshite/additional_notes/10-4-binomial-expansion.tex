  \section{the binomial expansion}
  hl p 58\\
  $(a+b)^n=nCr(n,0)\cdot a^n\cdot b^0+nCr(n,1)\cdot a^{n-1}\cdot b^1$\\
  $... + nCr(n,n)\cdot a^{n-n}\cdot b^{n}$\\
  $=\sum_{r=0}^{n}(nCr(n,r)\cdot a^{n-r}\cdot b^r)$\\
  $nCr(n,r)$ by GDC or Pascal's triangle.\\
$
